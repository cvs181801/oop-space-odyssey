YEAR 3001

The human race has detected an anomaly in a nearby star formation nebula. It's a bit merkabah-esque in form, and emitting a powerful energy within a membranous clear globe. Scientists are unsure of it's composition. Meanwhile, you're on a misson to deliver a fresh batch of new residents to the mars colony. Humanity has evolved to a point beyond war, hunger, and violence, but no recent major scientific discoveries have been made - until now. The temptation to explore this brilliant gaseous structure is strong...
